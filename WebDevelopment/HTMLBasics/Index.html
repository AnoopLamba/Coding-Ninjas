Five tips for front end development

Dont you just love exploring beautiful and neat sites with clean user interface?

However, it is not impossible either. Here are five tips that will help.

  1. Commenting

    Commenting is one such practice that is often ignored by programmers, especially for codes that are written by multiple programmers. But remember, commenting is an important part of project you create. With proper comments and organised files its always easier for you or others to jump in and understand where things are at and how they work.

  2. Invest in productive tools

    Invest in good productive tools for your project.

  3. Always be curious

    You have to be curious to try out new things for your project -

    • Frontend Focus
    • CSS weekly
    • JavaScript weekly
    • Web Design weekly

    Also, meke it a point to attend conferences and webinars.

  4. Refactor your code from time to time

    Keep refactoring your code from time to time to make your code look more good.

While these tips will surely help you become a good designer, in the long run, you must always remember designinf your platform - keep it simple and tidy.
